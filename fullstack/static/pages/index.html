"If it is broke, fix it"

"~ by Felipe Feldman, Patrick Hsu and Karan Shah ~"

As students, we understand that planning for course registration is often a hassle. Searching for courses that are specific to your degree, a certain professor, or a certain time slot can be quite difficult to find with the current system. So we decided to fix that by making Ali-B. We offer three main services: an exhaustive all-in-one search system for all things registration, a detailed course searching system, and an automatic schedule builder.

  • Campuses - Explore different campuses.
  • Degrees - View available degrees and their descriptions.
  • Departments - Browse academic departments.
  • Staff Directory - Find professors and their contact information.
  • Course Search - Search for courses based on various criteria.
  • Schedule Builder - Create your personalized course schedule.
  • Login - Log into your account.
